OWEN COOPER

About

Owen is a comedy writer and performer based in Yorkshire. He's been writing comedy since 2011 and has written jokes, sketches and additional material for lots of TV shows including Mock the Week (BBC2) and Celebrity Juice (ITV2); as well as children's shows such as The Dog Ate My Homework (CBBC).

He's also had material on BBC radio shows such as The Show What You Wrote (series 1 & 2, BBC Radio 4 Extra), Dave Gorman's Pub Olympics (BBC Radio 2) and Tom Deacon's Road Trip (BBC Radio 1).

In addition to writing for TV and radio, Owen acted in the TV sketch show Pop Slude (4 Music) and recently appeared in the radio show Follow the rabbit (BBC Radio 4). He's currently preparing his acceptance speech for the Oscars.
